Game Rules

Tic Tac Toe is a simple game played on a 3x3 grid. The goal is to get three of your symbols (X or O) in a row, column, or diagonal.

Players take turns placing their symbols on the grid. The first player to get three in a row wins. If all nine cells are filled and no player has three in a row, the game is a tie.
